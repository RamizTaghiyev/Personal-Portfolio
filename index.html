Unplugged 2026

Unity Indie Game  ·  XGB Studios

Awarded 1st place out of 13 teams at OYU GameJam 2026, sponsored by Xsolla, Nomadman Studios, IDGA, and Gamepons. Demoed at Game Summit 2025 as part of the Indie Games track.

Unplugged is a short 2D puzzle game about guiding a plug to its socket while avoiding obstacles and managing the cable’s limited length. The player controls the plug using mouse movement, plans the safest path, presses buttons to open doors, and solves small environmental puzzles to stay connected.

  • Designed around the GameJam theme of “connection” through plug-and-socket puzzle mechanics
  • Created cable-length based gameplay where the player must find the optimal route
  • Added obstacles, buttons, doors, and physics-based puzzle elements to create progressively harder levels

Smart Cargo 2024 – 2025

Senior Design Project  ·  ADA University  ·  Supervisor: Prof. John Burns

Intelligent, traffic-aware last-mile delivery platform built for Baku and beyond. Presented at ADA University Research & Development Showcase, May 2026.

Smart Cargo is a web-based logistics and delivery management platform developed as our Senior Design Project at ADA University. The system focuses on improving last-mile cargo delivery in Baku by helping users manage delivery data, analyze routes, and make more efficient route-planning decisions based on traffic and logistics conditions.

  • Web application for managing cargo deliveries and last-mile logistics
  • Uses traffic and delivery data to suggest more efficient routes
  • Helps reduce delays by supporting smarter route planning and delivery organization
